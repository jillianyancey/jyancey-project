Food & Drink

Seattle is home to many seasonal and year-round neighborhood farmers markets. Your family will enjoy gourmet food, fresh meat and produce, and live entertainment. Shop a wide selection of vintage clothing, sauces and candies, and handmade jewelry, soaps, and candles. Check out this month's featured farmers markets, and spend a few hours experiencing the work of local artists.
