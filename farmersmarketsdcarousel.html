Farmers Markets

Seattle is home to many seasonal and year-round neighborhood farmers markets. Your family will enjoy gourmet food, fresh meat and produce, and live entertainment. Shop a wide selection of vintage clothing, sauces and candies, and handmade jewelry, soaps, and candles. Check out this month's featured farmers markets, and spend a few hours experiencing the work of local artists.

MARKET HIGHLIGHTS

Fresh flowers and seasonal produce
Fresh fruits and vegetables
Gifts and vintage clothing

Featured Markets

MAGNOLIA MARKET

Dates and Times

10:00 AM-2:00 PM on Saturdays, June 3 through October 14, 2023 (plus additional Harvest Market on November 18, 2023)

Location and Parking

West McGraw Street and 33rd Avenue West (in Magnolia Village). Free street parking nearby and bike parking in Magnolia Village. Bus route 24 from Downtown Seattle.

Additional Information

ADA accessible market. All vendors accept cash, credit, and debit. Part of Seattle Neighborhood Farmers Markets.

SLU MARKET

Dates and Times

11:00 AM-4:00 PM on Saturdays, May 6 through September 30, 2023

Location and Parking

139 9th Avenue North. Next door to Denny Park in South Lake Union neighborhood. Parking on street and in nearby parking garages.

Additional Information

Downtown Seattle's largest street market. Over 65 booths and food trucks (including treats, crafts, and vintage clothing). DJs perform in the park.

CAPITOL HILL MARKET

Dates and Times

11:00 AM-3:00 PM on Sundays, year- round; 3:00 PM-7:00 PM on Tuesdays, July 11 through September 26, 2023

Location and Parking

East Denny Way between Broadway and 10th Avenue East. Free street parking on Sundays. ADA Parking on 11th near intersection with Denny (SE corner).

Additional Information

ADA accessible market. All vendors accept cash, credit, and debit. Part of Seattle Neighborhood Farmers Markets.
