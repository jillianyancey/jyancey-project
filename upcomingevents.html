Upcoming Events

Seattle hosts a wide variety of fun and meaningful events all year round, including festivals, exhibitions, concerts, cultural celebrations, sporting events, and more. Events are held at the Seattle Center and at many other locations around the city. Check out a few of the exciting, family-friendly events that are coming to Seattle this summer.

Featured Events

SEAFAIR SUMMER FOURTH

Dates and Times

All day on Tuesday, July 14, 2023. Fireworks start at 10:15 P.M.

Location

Gasworks Park and South Lake Union Park

About the Event

Fully choreographed fireworks presentation accompanied by music.

Free activities all day before the show. Voted one of nation's best fireworks shows by USA TODAY and Business Insider. Event is free and open to the public with free and reserved seating options available. For more information, visit Seafair Summer Fourth.

BITE OF SEATTLE

Dates and Times

Friday, July 21, through Sunday, July 23, 2023. Friday and Saturday hours are 10:00 A.M.-9 P.M. Sunday hours are 10:00 A.M.-8:00 P.M.

Location

Seattle Center

About the Event

Seattle's premium food festival returns after a 3-year break.

Free event! Experience 200 of the region's finest restaurants and specialty food purveyors and over 50 live music performances. Attendees can now order directly from their phones using CHEQ technology. This year is the 38th anniversary of Bite of Seattle. For more information, visit Bite of Seattle 2023.

BUMBERSHOOT

Dates and Times

Saturday, September 2, through Sunday, September 3, 2023.

Location

Seattle Center

About the Event

Celebrate Bumbershoot's 50th anniversary at this year's arts and music festival.

Experience this year's music and artist lineup, roller skating, extreme pogo stick, make-up artists, cat circus, gourmet food, and more! Children under 10 are free with accompanied adults (2 free children's tickets included with each ticket purchase order). For more information, visit Bumbershoot 2023.
