Parks & Play

The Seattle area is home to many beautiful parks and playgrounds, including many parks that feature inclusive play equipment for children of all abilities. Check out one of our favorite parks, and spend a few hours enjoying the Pacifc Northwest with your family. Pack a picnic and prepare for a fun, outdoor adventure!

Featured Inclusive Parks

Inspiration Playground

Location

10201 NE 4th St., Bellevue, WA

About the Park

Inspiration Playground is an innovative play space where children of all ages and abilities can explore. The park is open year round. For more information, visit Inspiration Playground.

Juanita Beach Park

Location

9703 NE Juanita Dr., Kirkland, WA

About the Park

Juanita Beach Park has an accessible playground with a level turf play surface and adaptive swings. For more information, visit Juanita Beach Park.

Meadow Crest Park

Location

3000 NE 16th St., Renton, WA

About the Park

Meadow Crest Park features a one-acre accessible playground that includes sensory activities and a caterpillar sculpture. For more information, visit Meadow Crest Park.
